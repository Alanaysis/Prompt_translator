## 详细条目

### MVP
**中文**: 产品最简单的版本，可以发布以测试核心假设并收集用户反馈

**English**: The simplest version of a product that can be released to test core hypotheses and gather user feedback

**提示**: MVP = minimum version to test if users want it. '最小可行产品'. NOT prototype (原型) - MVP is shipped to real users.

**示例**: "先做 MVP 验证一下" → 先做最简单的版本发布出去，看看用户是否真的需要这个功能

---

### PMF
**中文**: 产品满足强烈市场需求的程度——当你发现无法跟上需求增长的那一刻

**English**: The degree to which a product satisfies strong market demand — the moment you realize you can't keep up with demand

**提示**: PMF = product fits market need. '产品市场契合'. Pre-PMF = searching for demand. Post-PMF = scaling what works.

**示例**: "还没找到 PMF" → 产品还没找到足够多的愿意付费/持续使用的用户群体

---

### User Story
**中文**: 从最终用户角度描述功能的简短叙述（作为[用户]，我想要[目标]，以便[获得价值]）

**English**: A short, simple description of a feature told from the perspective of the end user (As a [user], I want [goal] so that [benefit])

**提示**: User story = feature described from user perspective. Format: 'As a [role], I want [feature], so that [benefit]'.

**示例**: "写个用户故事" → 用「作为X，我想要Y，以便Z」的格式描述需求

---

### Scoping
**中文**: 定义项目或发布中包含和不包含内容的边界

**English**: Defining the boundaries of what will and won't be included in a project or release

**提示**: Scoping = defining what's in/out of a project. 'Scope creep' = scope gradually expanding. '范围界定'.

**示例**: "先 scope 一下" → 先确定这次要做哪些内容、不做哪些内容

---

### Stakeholder
**中文**: 对项目结果有兴趣或受其影响的人（用户、投资人、团队、管理层）

**English**: Anyone who has an interest in or is affected by the project's outcome (users, investors, team, management)

**提示**: Stakeholder = anyone affected by or interested in the project. '利益相关者'/'干系人'. '对齐stakeholder' = align expectations.

**示例**: "需要对齐一下 stakeholder" → 需要跟所有相关方确认目标和期望是否一致

---

### AB Testing
**中文**: 对比两个版本（A 和 B）以确定哪个在特定指标上表现更好的实验

**English**: An experiment where two versions (A and B) are compared to see which performs better on a specific metric

**提示**: A/B testing = compare two versions with real users. 'A/B测试'. Needs enough traffic for statistical significance.

**示例**: "做个 AB 测试看看哪个方案好" → 同时上线两个版本，用数据决定最终用哪个

---

### Conversion Rate
**中文**: 完成期望操作（购买、注册等）的用户占总访问者的百分比

**English**: The percentage of users who complete a desired action (purchase, sign up, etc.) out of total visitors

**提示**: Conversion rate = % of visitors who do what you want. '转化率'. Track by funnel stage.

**示例**: "转化率太低了" → 来访问的人多，但真正注册/购买的人太少

---

### Pain Point
**中文**: 用户遇到的具体问题或挫败感，代表了产品解决方案的机会

**English**: A specific problem or frustration that users experience, representing an opportunity for a product solution

**提示**: Pain point = what frustrates users. '痛点'. Product solves pain points. Find them through user research.

**示例**: "用户痛点是什么？" → 用户在什么场景下感到最痛苦/不方便？这就是产品要解决的问题

---

### Value Proposition
**中文**: 清晰阐述产品为目标用户提供的独特价值，以及用户为什么应该选择它

**English**: A clear statement of the unique value a product delivers to its target users and why they should choose it

**提示**: Value proposition = why users should care. '价值主张'. Answer: what unique value do you deliver?

**示例**: "我们的价值主张是什么？" → 用户为什么要用我们的产品而不是竞品？我们提供什么独特价值？

---

### Funnel
**中文**: 描述用户从认知到转化各阶段的模型，每个阶段都有用户流失

**English**: A model representing the stages a user goes through from awareness to conversion, where drop-off occurs at each stage

**提示**: Funnel = stages from awareness to conversion. '漏斗'. Users drop off at each stage. Find biggest drop-off.

**示例**: "看看漏斗哪一步流失最多" → 分析转化流程中哪个环节的用户流失率最高

---

### Growth Hacking
**中文**: 通过在营销渠道和产品开发上快速实验，找到最有效增长方式的策略

**English**: A strategy focused on rapid experimentation across marketing channels and product development to find the most effective ways to grow

**提示**: Growth hacking = rapid experimentation for growth. '增长黑客'. Data-driven, creative, often low-cost tactics.

**示例**: "需要一些增长黑客的手段" → 需要通过创意和数据驱动的方法来快速增长用户

---

### Retention
**中文**: 在给定时间段内继续使用产品的用户百分比

**English**: The percentage of users who continue using a product over a given time period

**提示**: Retention = users coming back. '留存'. Key metrics: D1 (next day), D7, D30 retention rates.

**示例**: "次日留存多少？" → 用户第一天使用后，第二天还有多少人回来使用？

---

### Churn
**中文**: 在给定时间段内停止使用产品的用户百分比

**English**: The percentage of users who stop using a product over a given time period

**提示**: Churn = users leaving. '流失'. Opposite of retention. High churn = product not keeping users.

**示例**: "这个月 churn 太高了" → 这个月流失的用户太多了，需要分析原因

---

### Backlog
**中文**: 由产品负责人维护的、按优先级排列的功能、缺陷和任务列表

**English**: A prioritized list of features, bugs, and tasks to be worked on, maintained by the product owner

**提示**: Backlog = prioritized to-do list. '待办列表'/'需求池'. Managed by product owner in agile/scrum.

**示例**: "加到 backlog 里" → 先记下来放到需求池里，后面再排优先级

---

### Prioritization
**中文**: 根据价值、工作量和紧迫度决定功能或任务的开发顺序

**English**: The process of deciding the order in which features or tasks should be worked on based on value, effort, and urgency

**提示**: Prioritization = decide what to build first. Methods: RICE, MoSCoW, Impact/Effort matrix. '排优先级'.

**示例**: "先排一下优先级" → 评估各个需求的重要程度，决定先做哪个后做哪个

---

### Sprint
**中文**: 团队完成一组工作项的固定时间段（通常 1-4 周）

**English**: A fixed time period (usually 1-4 weeks) in which a team completes a set of work items

**提示**: Sprint = fixed time box for work. '迭代'/'冲刺'. Scrum concept, usually 2 weeks.

**示例**: "这个 sprint 做不完" → 当前迭代周期内无法完成这个需求

---

### Scrum
**中文**: 管理复杂工作的敏捷框架，包含角色（PO、SM、开发团队）、事件（迭代、站会、回顾）和工件

**English**: An agile framework for managing complex work, with roles (PO, SM, Dev Team), events (Sprint, Standup, Retro), and artifacts

**提示**: Scrum = agile framework with sprints, standups, retros. Roles: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Dev Team.

**示例**: "我们用 Scrum 流程" → 按照 Scrum 框架组织开发，有固定的迭代周期和仪式

---

### Standup
**中文**: 团队成员每天简短分享：昨天做了什么、今天要做什么、有什么阻碍

**English**: A short daily meeting where team members share what they did, what they'll do, and any blockers

**提示**: Standup = daily short sync meeting. '站会'. Three questions: done? planned? blockers? Usually 15 min max.

**示例**: "站会上说一下进度" → 在每日站会中汇报工作进展和阻塞问题

---

### Retrospective
**中文**: 迭代结束时的会议，团队反思哪些做得好、哪些不好、如何改进

**English**: A meeting at the end of a sprint where the team reflects on what went well, what didn't, and how to improve

**提示**: Retro = reflect and improve. '回顾会'/'复盘'. Format: what went well, what didn't, action items.

**示例**: "回顾一下这个 sprint" → 开复盘会讨论这个迭代的经验教训

---

### Kanban
**中文**: 使用列（待办、进行中、已完成）和 WIP 限制的可视化工作流管理方法

**English**: A visual workflow management method using columns (To Do, In Progress, Done) with WIP limits

**提示**: Kanban = visual workflow with columns. '看板'. WIP limits prevent overload. Continuous flow, no fixed sprints.

**示例**: "用看板管理任务" → 把任务卡片在看板上从左到右移动，可视化工作流

---

### OKR
**中文**: 目标设定框架：目标（O）定义要达成什么，关键结果（KR）衡量如何到达

**English**: A goal-setting framework: Objectives define what to achieve, Key Results measure how to get there

**提示**: OKR = Objectives + Key Results. '目标与关键结果'. Objective = ambitious goal. Key Result = measurable outcome. Google's framework.

**示例**: "Q2 的 OKR 是什么？" → 这个季度的目标和关键结果是什么？

---

### KPI
**中文**: 用于评估战略目标达成情况的可量化指标

**English**: Quantifiable metrics used to evaluate success against strategic objectives

**提示**: KPI = measurable success metric. '关键绩效指标'. Lagging indicator. OKR is more ambitious, KPI is more operational.

**示例**: "这个季度的 KPI 是 DAU 增长 20%" → 核心考核指标是日活用户增长 20%

---

### North Star Metric
**中文**: 最能体现产品为客户交付的核心价值的单一指标

**English**: The single metric that best captures the core value your product delivers to customers

**提示**: North Star Metric = one metric that captures core value. '北极星指标'. Example: Airbnb = nights booked, Spotify = time listened.

**示例**: "我们的北极星指标是周活跃用户数" → 最核心的指标是每周有多少用户在使用产品

---

### Roadmap
**中文**: 产品愿景和方向的高级可视化概览

**English**: A high-level visual summary of the vision and direction of a product over time

**提示**: Roadmap = high-level product plan over time. '路线图'. Themes/goals, not specific features. Adjusts as you learn.

**示例**: "看一下 Q3 的路线图" → 查看第三季度的产品规划和目标

---

### Wireframe
**中文**: UI 布局的低保真可视化表示，展示结构而非详细设计

**English**: A low-fidelity visual representation of a UI layout, showing structure without detailed design

**提示**: Wireframe = low-fi UI sketch. '线框图'. Structure only, no colors/details. Tools: Figma, Balsamiq, paper sketch.

**示例**: "先画个线框图" → 在开始设计前先用线框图确定页面布局和元素位置

---

### Prototype
**中文**: 产品的交互模拟，用于在开发前测试和验证设计决策

**English**: An interactive simulation of a product used for testing and validating design decisions before development

**提示**: Prototype = interactive mockup for testing. '原型'. High-fi = looks real. Low-fi = wireframe. Test before building.

**示例**: "做个原型测试一下" → 做一个可交互的原型让用户试用，验证设计是否合理

---

### Usability
**中文**: 用户完成目标的容易程度和直觉性

**English**: How easy and intuitive a product is for users to accomplish their goals

**提示**: Usability = how easy to use. '可用性'/'易用性'. Measured by: task completion rate, time on task, error rate.

**示例**: "可用性测试发现用户找不到按钮" → 用户测试显示按钮位置不直观，需要调整

---

### DAU
**中文**: 一天内使用产品的独立用户数量

**English**: The number of unique users who engage with a product in a day

**提示**: DAU = daily active users. '日活跃用户'. Key engagement metric. DAU/MAU ratio = stickiness.

**示例**: "DAU 破万了" → 日活跃用户数超过一万

---

### MAU
**中文**: 一个月内使用产品的独立用户数量

**English**: The number of unique users who engage with a product in a month

**提示**: MAU = monthly active users. '月活跃用户'. Broader reach metric. DAU/MAU = engagement ratio.

**示例**: "MAU 100 万" → 月活跃用户达到 100 万

---

### NPS
**中文**: 基于用户推荐意愿（0-10 分）衡量客户忠诚度的指标

**English**: A metric measuring customer loyalty based on how likely they are to recommend the product (0-10 scale)

**提示**: NPS = Net Promoter Score (0-10). '净推荐值'. Promoters (9-10) - Detractors (0-6) = NPS. >50 is excellent.

**示例**: "NPS 评分多少？" → 用户推荐意愿评分是多少？9-10 是推荐者，0-6 是贬损者

---

### Bounce Rate
**中文**: 只浏览一个页面就离开网站的访客百分比

**English**: The percentage of visitors who leave a site after viewing only one page

**提示**: Bounce Rate = % visitors who leave after one page. '跳出率'. High = landing page not engaging. Improve: better CTA, faster load.

**示例**: "跳出率太高了" → 用户进来就走了，没有继续浏览，说明落地页有问题

---

### Onboarding
**中文**: 引导新用户理解并从产品中获得价值的过程

**English**: The process of guiding new users to understand and get value from a product

**提示**: Onboarding = guide new users to value. '新手引导'. Key moment: first 'aha' experience. Reduce time-to-value.

**示例**: "优化一下新手引导流程" → 改进新用户首次使用产品的体验，帮助他们快速上手

---

### Activation
**中文**: 新用户首次体验到产品核心价值的时刻

**English**: The point where a new user first experiences the core value of a product

**提示**: Activation = user first experiences core value. '激活'. Define your activation moment. Example: 'sent first message' for chat app.

**示例**: "激活率太低了" → 注册的用户中真正使用了核心功能的比例太低

---

### AARRR
**中文**: 创业公司指标框架：获取、激活、留存、收入、推荐

**English**: Startup metrics framework: Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Revenue, Referral

**提示**: AARRR = Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Revenue, Referral. '海盗指标'. Dave McClure's startup metrics framework.

**示例**: "用 AARRR 模型分析" → 按获取→激活→留存→收入→推荐的漏斗分析用户行为

---

### Viral Loop
**中文**: 现有用户自然邀请新用户的机制，产生指数级增长

**English**: A mechanism where existing users naturally invite new users, creating exponential growth

**提示**: Viral Loop = users invite users. '病毒循环'. K-factor > 1 = viral growth. Examples: referral rewards, shareable content.

**示例**: "设计一个病毒传播机制" → 让用户自发分享和邀请，实现用户自增长

---

### Product-Led Growth
**中文**: 由产品本身驱动获客、转化和扩展的增长策略

**English**: A growth strategy where the product itself drives acquisition, conversion, and expansion

**提示**: PLG = product drives growth, not sales team. '产品驱动增长'. Examples: Slack, Notion, Figma. Free tier → upgrade.

**示例**: "我们走 PLG 路线" → 靠产品本身的体验和口碑来获客增长，而不是靠销售团队

---

### Freemium
**中文**: 基础功能免费、高级功能付费的定价模型

**English**: A pricing model where basic features are free, but advanced features require payment

**提示**: Freemium = free + premium. '免费增值'. Free tier for acquisition, paid tier for revenue. Key: free must be valuable enough.

**示例**: "用 freemium 模式" → 提供免费版本吸引用户，部分高级功能需要付费

---

### Persona
**中文**: 基于研究数据创建的、代表关键用户群体的虚构角色

**English**: A fictional character representing a key user segment, based on research data

**提示**: Persona = fictional user representative. '用户画像'. Based on real research. Includes goals, frustrations, behaviors.

**示例**: "我们的核心 persona 是什么？" → 我们的主要用户画像是什么样的人？

---

### Jobs to be Done
**中文**: 关注客户想要完成什么「任务」而非产品功能的框架

**English**: A framework focusing on what 'job' a customer is trying to accomplish, rather than product features

**提示**: JTBD = what job is user hiring your product for? '待完成任务'. Focus on motivation, not features. Clayton Christensen's framework.

**示例**: "用户 hire 这个产品是为了解决什么 JTBD？" → 用户选择这个产品是为了完成什么任务？
