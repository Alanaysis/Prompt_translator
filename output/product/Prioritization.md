# Prioritization

**别名**: 优先级排序, 排优先级, 需求优先级

## 定义

**中文**: 根据价值、工作量和紧迫度决定功能或任务的开发顺序

**English**: The process of deciding the order in which features or tasks should be worked on based on value, effort, and urgency

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Prioritization = decide what to build first. Methods: RICE, MoSCoW, Impact/Effort matrix. '排优先级'.

## 示例

- "先排一下优先级"
  → 评估各个需求的重要程度，决定先做哪个后做哪个
