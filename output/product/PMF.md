# PMF

**别名**: 产品市场契合, Product-Market Fit

## 定义

**中文**: 产品满足强烈市场需求的程度——当你发现无法跟上需求增长的那一刻

**English**: The degree to which a product satisfies strong market demand — the moment you realize you can't keep up with demand

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: PMF = product fits market need. '产品市场契合'. Pre-PMF = searching for demand. Post-PMF = scaling what works.

## 示例

- "还没找到 PMF"
  → 产品还没找到足够多的愿意付费/持续使用的用户群体
