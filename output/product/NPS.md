# NPS

**别名**: 净推荐值, Net Promoter Score

## 定义

**中文**: 基于用户推荐意愿（0-10 分）衡量客户忠诚度的指标

**English**: A metric measuring customer loyalty based on how likely they are to recommend the product (0-10 scale)

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: NPS = Net Promoter Score (0-10). '净推荐值'. Promoters (9-10) - Detractors (0-6) = NPS. >50 is excellent.

## 示例

- "NPS 评分多少？"
  → 用户推荐意愿评分是多少？9-10 是推荐者，0-6 是贬损者
