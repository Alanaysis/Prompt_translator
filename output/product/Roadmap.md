# Roadmap

**别名**: 路线图, 产品路线图, 产品规划

## 定义

**中文**: 产品愿景和方向的高级可视化概览

**English**: A high-level visual summary of the vision and direction of a product over time

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Roadmap = high-level product plan over time. '路线图'. Themes/goals, not specific features. Adjusts as you learn.

## 示例

- "看一下 Q3 的路线图"
  → 查看第三季度的产品规划和目标

## 关联术语

- backlog
- prioritization
- strategy
