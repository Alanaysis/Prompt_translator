# Viral Loop

**别名**: 病毒循环, 病毒传播, 自传播

## 定义

**中文**: 现有用户自然邀请新用户的机制，产生指数级增长

**English**: A mechanism where existing users naturally invite new users, creating exponential growth

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Viral Loop = users invite users. '病毒循环'. K-factor > 1 = viral growth. Examples: referral rewards, shareable content.

## 示例

- "设计一个病毒传播机制"
  → 让用户自发分享和邀请，实现用户自增长

## 关联术语

- growth-hacking
- referral
- K-factor
