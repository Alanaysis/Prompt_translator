# Conversion Rate

**别名**: 转化率, 转化, Conversion

## 定义

**中文**: 完成期望操作（购买、注册等）的用户占总访问者的百分比

**English**: The percentage of users who complete a desired action (purchase, sign up, etc.) out of total visitors

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Conversion rate = % of visitors who do what you want. '转化率'. Track by funnel stage.

## 示例

- "转化率太低了"
  → 来访问的人多，但真正注册/购买的人太少
