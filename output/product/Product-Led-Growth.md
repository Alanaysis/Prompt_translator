# Product-Led Growth

**别名**: 产品驱动增长, PLG

## 定义

**中文**: 由产品本身驱动获客、转化和扩展的增长策略

**English**: A growth strategy where the product itself drives acquisition, conversion, and expansion

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: PLG = product drives growth, not sales team. '产品驱动增长'. Examples: Slack, Notion, Figma. Free tier → upgrade.

## 示例

- "我们走 PLG 路线"
  → 靠产品本身的体验和口碑来获客增长，而不是靠销售团队

## 关联术语

- freemium
- self-serve
