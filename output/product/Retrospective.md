# Retrospective

**别名**: 回顾会, 复盘, Retro, Sprint Retro

## 定义

**中文**: 迭代结束时的会议，团队反思哪些做得好、哪些不好、如何改进

**English**: A meeting at the end of a sprint where the team reflects on what went well, what didn't, and how to improve

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Retro = reflect and improve. '回顾会'/'复盘'. Format: what went well, what didn't, action items.

## 示例

- "回顾一下这个 sprint"
  → 开复盘会讨论这个迭代的经验教训
