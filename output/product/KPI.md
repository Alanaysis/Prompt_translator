# KPI

**别名**: 关键绩效指标, Key Performance Indicator

## 定义

**中文**: 用于评估战略目标达成情况的可量化指标

**English**: Quantifiable metrics used to evaluate success against strategic objectives

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: KPI = measurable success metric. '关键绩效指标'. Lagging indicator. OKR is more ambitious, KPI is more operational.

## 示例

- "这个季度的 KPI 是 DAU 增长 20%"
  → 核心考核指标是日活用户增长 20%
