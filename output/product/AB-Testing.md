# AB Testing

**别名**: A/B测试, 分流测试, 对比测试

## 定义

**中文**: 对比两个版本（A 和 B）以确定哪个在特定指标上表现更好的实验

**English**: An experiment where two versions (A and B) are compared to see which performs better on a specific metric

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: A/B testing = compare two versions with real users. 'A/B测试'. Needs enough traffic for statistical significance.

## 示例

- "做个 AB 测试看看哪个方案好"
  → 同时上线两个版本，用数据决定最终用哪个
