# Bounce Rate

**别名**: 跳出率, 弹出率

## 定义

**中文**: 只浏览一个页面就离开网站的访客百分比

**English**: The percentage of visitors who leave a site after viewing only one page

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Bounce Rate = % visitors who leave after one page. '跳出率'. High = landing page not engaging. Improve: better CTA, faster load.

## 示例

- "跳出率太高了"
  → 用户进来就走了，没有继续浏览，说明落地页有问题
