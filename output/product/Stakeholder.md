# Stakeholder

**别名**: 利益相关者, 干系人, 相关方

## 定义

**中文**: 对项目结果有兴趣或受其影响的人（用户、投资人、团队、管理层）

**English**: Anyone who has an interest in or is affected by the project's outcome (users, investors, team, management)

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Stakeholder = anyone affected by or interested in the project. '利益相关者'/'干系人'. '对齐stakeholder' = align expectations.

## 示例

- "需要对齐一下 stakeholder"
  → 需要跟所有相关方确认目标和期望是否一致

## 关联术语

- requirements
- alignment
- communication
