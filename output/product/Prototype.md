# Prototype

**别名**: 原型, 交互原型, 高保真原型

## 定义

**中文**: 产品的交互模拟，用于在开发前测试和验证设计决策

**English**: An interactive simulation of a product used for testing and validating design decisions before development

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Prototype = interactive mockup for testing. '原型'. High-fi = looks real. Low-fi = wireframe. Test before building.

## 示例

- "做个原型测试一下"
  → 做一个可交互的原型让用户试用，验证设计是否合理
