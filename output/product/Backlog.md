# Backlog

**别名**: 待办列表, 需求池, Backlog

## 定义

**中文**: 由产品负责人维护的、按优先级排列的功能、缺陷和任务列表

**English**: A prioritized list of features, bugs, and tasks to be worked on, maintained by the product owner

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Backlog = prioritized to-do list. '待办列表'/'需求池'. Managed by product owner in agile/scrum.

## 示例

- "加到 backlog 里"
  → 先记下来放到需求池里，后面再排优先级
