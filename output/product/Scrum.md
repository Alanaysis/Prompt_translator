# Scrum

**别名**: Scrum 框架, 敏捷开发框架

## 定义

**中文**: 管理复杂工作的敏捷框架，包含角色（PO、SM、开发团队）、事件（迭代、站会、回顾）和工件

**English**: An agile framework for managing complex work, with roles (PO, SM, Dev Team), events (Sprint, Standup, Retro), and artifacts

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Scrum = agile framework with sprints, standups, retros. Roles: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Dev Team.

## 示例

- "我们用 Scrum 流程"
  → 按照 Scrum 框架组织开发，有固定的迭代周期和仪式

## 关联术语

- sprint
- agile
- standup
