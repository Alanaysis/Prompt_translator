# Pain Point

**别名**: 痛点, 用户痛点, 需求痛点

## 定义

**中文**: 用户遇到的具体问题或挫败感，代表了产品解决方案的机会

**English**: A specific problem or frustration that users experience, representing an opportunity for a product solution

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Pain point = what frustrates users. '痛点'. Product solves pain points. Find them through user research.

## 示例

- "用户痛点是什么？"
  → 用户在什么场景下感到最痛苦/不方便？这就是产品要解决的问题
