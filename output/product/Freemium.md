# Freemium

**别名**: 免费增值, 免费+付费

## 定义

**中文**: 基础功能免费、高级功能付费的定价模型

**English**: A pricing model where basic features are free, but advanced features require payment

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Freemium = free + premium. '免费增值'. Free tier for acquisition, paid tier for revenue. Key: free must be valuable enough.

## 示例

- "用 freemium 模式"
  → 提供免费版本吸引用户，部分高级功能需要付费
