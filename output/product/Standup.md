# Standup

**别名**: 站会, 每日站会, Daily Standup, Daily

## 定义

**中文**: 团队成员每天简短分享：昨天做了什么、今天要做什么、有什么阻碍

**English**: A short daily meeting where team members share what they did, what they'll do, and any blockers

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Standup = daily short sync meeting. '站会'. Three questions: done? planned? blockers? Usually 15 min max.

## 示例

- "站会上说一下进度"
  → 在每日站会中汇报工作进展和阻塞问题
