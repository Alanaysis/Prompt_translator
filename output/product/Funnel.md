# Funnel

**别名**: 漏斗, 转化漏斗, 用户漏斗, Conversion Funnel

## 定义

**中文**: 描述用户从认知到转化各阶段的模型，每个阶段都有用户流失

**English**: A model representing the stages a user goes through from awareness to conversion, where drop-off occurs at each stage

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Funnel = stages from awareness to conversion. '漏斗'. Users drop off at each stage. Find biggest drop-off.

## 示例

- "看看漏斗哪一步流失最多"
  → 分析转化流程中哪个环节的用户流失率最高

## 关联术语

- conversion-rate
- drop-off
- acquisition
