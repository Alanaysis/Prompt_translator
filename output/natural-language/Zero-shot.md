# Zero-shot

**别名**: 零样本, 零样本学习

## 定义

**中文**: 在提示词中不提供任何示例就让模型执行任务

**English**: Asking a model to perform a task without providing any examples in the prompt

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Zero-shot = no examples in prompt. '零样本'. Simpler prompt, relies on model's pre-training. Use when task is straightforward.

## 示例

- "zero-shot 试试看"
  → 不给例子，直接让模型完成任务，看效果如何
