# Streaming

**别名**: 流式输出, 流式响应, SSE

## 定义

**中文**: 逐个 token 生成并返回 AI 响应，而不是等待完整响应

**English**: Delivering AI responses token by token as they're generated, rather than waiting for the complete response

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Streaming = deliver tokens as generated. '流式输出'. Reduces perceived latency. Users see response forming in real-time.

## 示例

- "用流式输出"
  → 逐字显示 AI 的回复，而不是等全部生成完再显示

- latency

- user-experience
