# Fine-tuning

**别名**: 微调, 模型微调, Fine-tune

## 定义

**中文**: 在预训练模型基础上用特定领域数据进一步训练，以提升特定任务的表现

**English**: Further training a pre-trained model on domain-specific data to improve performance for specific tasks

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Fine-tuning = further train model on specific data. '微调'. Cheaper than training from scratch. Use when prompts aren't enough.

## 示例

- "需要微调一下模型"
  → 用领域数据进一步训练通用模型，使其更擅长特定任务

## 关联术语

- pre-training
- transfer-learning
- LLM
