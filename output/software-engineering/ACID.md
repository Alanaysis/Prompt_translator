# ACID

**别名**: 事务特性, ACID 特性

## 定义

**中文**: 保证数据库事务可靠性的四个特性：原子性、一致性、隔离性、持久性

**English**: Properties guaranteeing reliable database transactions: Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Durability

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: ACID = Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Durability. '事务特性'. Guarantees reliable transactions.

## 示例

- "这个操作要保证 ACID"
  → 数据库操作必须满足事务的四个特性
