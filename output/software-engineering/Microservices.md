# Microservices

**别名**: 微服务, 微服务架构, Microservice Architecture

## 定义

**中文**: 一种架构风格，将应用拆分为多个小型、可独立部署的服务，通过网络通信协作

**English**: Architectural style where an application is composed of small, independently deployable services that communicate over a network

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Microservices = many small services vs monolith = one big app. '拆微服务' = decompose into microservices.

## 示例

- "我们要拆成微服务"
  → 要把现有的单体应用拆分成多个独立的小服务
