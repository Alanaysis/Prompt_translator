# Authentication

**别名**: 认证, 身份验证, 鉴权, Auth

## 定义

**中文**: 验证用户或系统身份的过程

**English**: The process of verifying the identity of a user or system

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Authentication = verify identity (who are you?). '认证'/'身份验证'. Different from Authorization (what can you do?).

## 示例

- "这个接口需要认证"
  → 调用前必须先验证身份（登录）
