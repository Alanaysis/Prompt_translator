# Load Balancer

**别名**: 负载均衡, 负载均衡器, LB

## 定义

**中文**: 将网络流量分配到多台服务器的设备或软件

**English**: A device or software that distributes network traffic across multiple servers

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Load Balancer = distribute traffic across servers. '负载均衡'. Algorithms: round-robin, least-connections, IP hash.

## 示例

- "前面加个负载均衡"
  → 在服务器集群前部署负载均衡器，分发请求
