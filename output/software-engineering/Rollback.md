# Rollback

**别名**: 回滚, 回退, 版本回退

## 定义

**中文**: 将系统或部署恢复到之前已知正常的状态

**English**: Reverting a system or deployment to a previous known-good state

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Rollback = revert to previous version. '回滚' = rollback. Common when deployment breaks things.

## 示例

- "新版本有问题，先回滚"
  → 最新部署有 bug，先恢复到上一个稳定版本

## 关联术语

- deployment
- hotfix
- version-control
