# CDN

**别名**: 内容分发网络, Content Delivery Network, 加速

## 定义

**中文**: 地理分布的服务器网络，从最近的位置向用户分发内容

**English**: A geographically distributed network of servers that delivers content to users from the nearest location

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: CDN = content delivered from nearest server. '内容分发网络'. For static assets, reduces latency globally.

## 示例

- "静态资源上 CDN"
  → 把图片、JS、CSS 等静态文件放到 CDN 上加速访问
