# Observability

**别名**: 可观测性, 可观测, Telemetry

## 定义

**中文**: 通过系统的外部输出（日志、指标、链路追踪）来理解系统内部状态的能力

**English**: The ability to understand the internal state of a system from its external outputs (logs, metrics, traces)

> 💡 **Agent 提示**: Observability = understand system from outside. Three pillars: logs, metrics, traces. '可观测性'.

## 示例

- "服务可观测性不够"
  → 缺乏足够的日志、指标和追踪来排查问题

## 关联术语

- logging
- monitoring
- tracing
- metrics
